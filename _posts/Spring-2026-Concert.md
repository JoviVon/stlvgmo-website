"From Hollow to Grace" takes you on a musical odyssey through worlds of fantasy, adventure, and wonder. From the dark, atmospheric depths of Hollow Knight to the majestic grandeur of Elden Ring, this concert showcases the incredible artistry of video game music composition.

Whether you're a dedicated gamer, a music enthusiast, or simply looking for a unique concert experience, this performance promises to be an evening you won't forget.
